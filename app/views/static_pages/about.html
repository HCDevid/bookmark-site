We are a basement studio (coming to a garage near you), who watch alot of porn. We have new favorites every day, and we forget old favorites every day.


After complaining about how we couldn’t keep track of our porn easily and access it in moments of dire need, like when showing friends a great porn video. We thought, “Why not built a site that lets us favorite all our most-liked porn?”


The sky turned red and the earth shook at the power of the revelation. “If you build it, they will come”, spoke a thunderous voice from the sky. And we fell on our faces and thanked Providence for its inspiration.


Many ramen noodles later, this is our site.
